Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026 · Version 1.0 · IIT Patna Capstone-I Project
✓ YOUR PRIVACY IS FULLY PROTECTED

Healthlyzer does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Your symptom inputs are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We have no database, no user accounts, and no data retention of any kind.

Section 01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Healthlyzer, developed by Group 50 of IIT Patna's Capstone-I program, handles information. We are committed to full transparency — we collect nothing and store nothing.

Section 02

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (name, email, phone, address)
  • No health data or medical history beyond the current session's inputs
  • No IP addresses or device fingerprints
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics
  • No location data
  • No browsing history or usage patterns
  • No account registration or payment information
Section 03

How the Application Works

  • You select symptom checkboxes (fever, cough, headache, vomiting, fatigue)
  • These binary values are submitted via HTTP POST to our Flask server
  • The server passes values to the pre-trained Random Forest ML model
  • The model returns a prediction which is displayed to you
  • The session ends — no data is written to any database or file
Important: Your symptom selections exist only in memory during the request-response cycle and are permanently discarded immediately after the prediction is returned. There is no logging, no storage, and no transmission to third parties.

Section 05
